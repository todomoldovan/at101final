The force is a metaphysical and ubiquitous power in the fictional universe of Star Wars. It is wielded by characters in the franchise, often in the form of the lightsaber, a weapon used by the Jedi and the Sith.

Darth Vader appears in the original trilogy as a pivotal antagonist whose actions drive the plot, while his past as Anakin Skywalker and the story of his corruption are central to the narrative of the prequel trilogy.
