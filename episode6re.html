Episode VI

Return of the Jedi

Luke Skywalker has returned to his home planet of Tatooine for five reasons:

1. To rescue his friend Han Solo from the clutches of the vile gangster Jabba the Hutt.

2. Clues have been given that a new, heavily armored space station is being constructed, one that will be even more powerful than the first Death Star.

3. He learns that he is the Chosen One prophesied to bring balance to the galaxy.

4. The Rebels are being hunted down relentlessly.

5. And to visit his childhood friend, moisture farmer Owen Lars...
