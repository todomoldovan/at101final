Episode IV

tl;dr:

The film begins with the Rebel Alliance, a group of freedom fighters.

They oppose the Galactic Empire, a tyrannical regime led by Emperor Palpatine and his Sith apprentice Darth Vader, who has been tasked with constructing a new battle station.

The Rebels have stolen the plans to the Death Star.
