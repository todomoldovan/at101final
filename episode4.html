Theodora Moldovan

Theodora Moldovan is a sophomore at Connecticut College, where she is pursuing a major in Data Science, a minor in Government, and a certificate in the Ammerman Center for Arts and Technology.

Currently, Theodora serves as a teaching assistant and grader for the Computer Science Department and as a grader for the Mathematics and Statistics Department at Connecticut College. On campus, she's been involed with Women in STEM, the Student Survey Advisory Panel, Hillel and the International Student Association, as well as serving as an international student adviser for incoming first-year students. Theodora has experience in research through her participation in Polymath REU, where she worked on the "Hat Guessing Number of Graphs" project. She is looking forward to diving into the field of machine learning in the future.
